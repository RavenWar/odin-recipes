Korean Cucumber Salad

Description

This is the perfect recipe for making crispy, fresh, and refreshing Korean Cucumber Salad!

Ingredients

  • 1 English Cucumber
  • 1t Salt
  • 2T Red pepper flackes (gochugaru)
  • 1/2T Red pepper paste (gochujang)
  • 1t minced garlic
  • 1T Soy sauce
  • 1.5T Rice vinegar
  • 1t Sugar
  • 1/2T Sesame oil
  • 1t Whold sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Thinly slice cucumber into 1/8" pieces and place into a large bowl.
  2. Sprinkle salt over the cucumber and allow to rest for 30 minutes. This will remove some of the water from the cucumber and allow for crispy cucumbers.
  3. While the cucumber is resting, mix together gochujang, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and sesame oil into a small bowl.
  4. Remove cucumber from the bowl and dry with a paper towel. Dump the liquid in the bowl down the drain and put the cucumber back into the bowl.
  5. Pour the spice mixture over the cucumbers and gently toss until all cucumber pieces are coated. Serve and eat, or refrigerate to enjoy for up to two weeks.
